Nuxalk Language & Pronouncation

Nuxalk, also known as Bella Coola, is a Wakashan language, related to other languages like Makah and Kwakiutl. As an example, here are twenty basic Bella Coola words, sourced from Native Langauges Website

English Nuxalk
One Maw
Two Lhwaas
Three Asmús
Four Mús
Five Ts'ícw
Man Kwtmts
Woman Cnas
Sun Snx
Moon Tl'uk
Water Qla

Although the Nuxalk alphabet incorporates many of the same letters used in the English alphabet, the pronounciation of many letters are different, so please consult the audio files in the following link. You will find that the language sounds really cool!
